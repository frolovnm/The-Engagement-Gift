# Year 1 Anniversary Gifts: Paper & Traditional Ideas

Your first anniversary is a milestone worth celebrating! Traditionally, the first year calls for paper gifts, symbolizing the fragile yet precious nature of new marriage. But don't worry—paper gifts can be incredibly romantic and meaningful when chosen thoughtfully.

## Traditional Paper Gifts

### 1. Custom Love Letters Book
Collect handwritten letters from friends and family, expressing their wishes for your marriage. Bind them together in a beautiful book to treasure forever.

### 2. Personalized Coupon Book
Create a custom coupon book filled with promises—breakfast in bed, a free massage, a movie night of their choice. It's thoughtful and practical.

### 3. Framed Wedding Vows
Have your original wedding vows professionally calligraphed and framed. This becomes a beautiful wall piece that reminds you daily of your commitment.

### 4. Travel Adventure Book
Design a custom travel journal or "adventure bucket list" where you plan future trips together. Include tear-out pages for different destinations.

## Modern Paper Alternatives

If traditional paper gifts feel too simple, consider these modern spins:

### Photo Book or Album
Create a beautiful photo book of your engagement and wedding moments. Companies like Artifact Uprising and Chatbooks make stunning keepsakes.

### Newspaper from Your Wedding Day
Order a reprint of the newspaper from your wedding date. Frame it for a nostalgic touch.

### Printed Canvas Art
Turn your favorite wedding photo into a canvas print. It's more durable than paper but maintains the handmade sentiment.

## Experiential Gifts (Non-Paper)

If you want to go beyond paper, consider experiences that celebrate your first year:

- **Romantic weekend getaway** – A nearby bed & breakfast or hotel staycation
- **Couples massage or spa day** – Relax together after a hectic wedding season
- **Fine dining experience** – Book a reservation at a restaurant you've wanted to try
- **Ballroom dancing lessons** – Learn to dance together as a married couple

## TheEngagementGift Recommendation

For your first anniversary, combine a meaningful paper gift with a small experience. For example, pair handwritten love letters with a special dinner at home. The key is personalization—show that you've thought carefully about what your partner cherishes most.

Remember: it's not about the cost, it's about the sentiment. Your partner will treasure whatever you give them as long as it comes from the heart.
