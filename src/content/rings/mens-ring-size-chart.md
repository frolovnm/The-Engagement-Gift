Finding the right ring size for men can be tricky, especially when you want the ring to be a surprise. This comprehensive guide breaks down everything you need to know about men's ring sizes, from US to international conversions.

## Understanding Ring Sizes

Ring sizes are typically measured in US sizes, which range from 1 to 13 for men. However, international sizing can vary significantly. Here's what you need to know:

### US Ring Sizes
- **Small:** Sizes 6-8 (ring circumference 47-53mm)
- **Medium:** Sizes 9-11 (ring circumference 57-64mm)
- **Large:** Sizes 12-13 (ring circumference 68-73mm)

## How to Measure at Home

If you already have a ring that fits your partner perfectly, measuring it is the easiest approach. Use a ruler to measure the inside diameter and consult a sizing chart.

Alternatively, wrap a piece of string around your finger and mark where it overlaps. Measure that length against a ruler and use our conversion chart to find your size.

## Pro Tips for Ring Shopping

1. **Ring fit varies by width** – Wider bands may fit slightly smaller, so adjust up half a size
2. **Temperature matters** – Fingers are slightly larger in warm weather and smaller in cold
3. **Time of day counts** – Hands swell throughout the day, so measure in the afternoon
4. **Metal thickness** – The thickness of the band can affect how the ring sits on the finger

## The Ring Lab Recommendation

When in doubt, we recommend going with your partner's typical shoe size as a starting point for men's rings, though this is not a precise method. The most reliable approach is having a jeweler size your finger professionally.
